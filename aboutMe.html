Everytime growing up when I looked at who I was and who I wanted to become, I paid attention to what I was passionate about. From a young age, technology always seemed to fascinate me and I still remember to this day coming down the stairs in the early 90’s to a Macintosh computer under the Christmas tree. This is where my journey began.

Fast forward to college where I started as an undecided major, however all the classes I took focused around Computer Science. This was through my sophomore year when I suddenly decided I didn’t want to sit behind a computer for the rest of my life, thus declaring my major. While I’m grateful for my degree in Business Management and the 13+ years I spent in the finance industry as well as staffing, something has always been missing. Over the years I’d try to teach myself various coding languages but could never find the time or discipline to push through and get it done. That's when I decided the best way to succeed would be to devote myself to a structured environment and work with others to help persevere. This year, I graduated from a full stack bootcamp offered through the University of Pennsylvania and it was exactly what I needed. I learned so much but was particularly fond of front end development and a touch of back end.

I look forward to the years to come, continuing to follow my tech journey and learning new and upcoming technologies and practices. I look forward to learning from people who are better than me. I look forward to becoming a true full stack developer and hopefully repaying the favor to the younger generation by sharing my story and everything I’ve learned.
